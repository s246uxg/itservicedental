Our Story

Built by Dental-Tech
Professionals

IT Service Dental was founded in 2019 with a simple mission: provide dental practices in Northern Virginia with IT support that actually understands their business.

We saw too many dental practices struggling with general IT providers who didn't understand HIPAA compliance, dental software, or the unique workflow of a dental office. Patients were waiting longer, staff were frustrated, and practice owners were spending valuable time on technology problems.

Today, we support over 50 dental practices across Fairfax, Arlington, Alexandria, Reston, Vienna, McLean, and the greater Northern Virginia area. Our team combines deep IT expertise with specific knowledge of dental practice operations.
